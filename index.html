About CV Research Teaching

Full Professor of Economics at the Department of Economics and Management, University of Trento, Italy.

I earned my PhD in Economics and Management from the Sant’Anna School of Advanced Studies.

My research focuses on the following areas:

  • Industrial dynamics and firm heterogeneity
  • International trade and firm performance
  • Productivity and policy evaluation
  • Environmental trade policies and critical raw materials

Member of the Empirical Microeconomics Laboratory (EmiL).
